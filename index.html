WHO ARE WE

This society was found in 2013 as JSS Mobile Innovation Lab under Sourabh Verma and Prince Kumar along with the faculty coordinator Mr.Manoj Kumar. In 2014, name changed to Microsoft Mobile Innovation Lab. The society organizes the workshops to facilitate learning in the students.

We believe in saying no to thousands of projects, so that we can really focus on the few that are truly important and meaningful to us.

WHAT IS OUR VISION
